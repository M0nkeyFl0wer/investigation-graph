## Notes

The property was transferred from the Harbor City Redevelopment Authority to Meridian
Holdings at significantly below appraised value. The Redevelopment Authority board
approved the sale in a 3-2 vote. Board member James Whitfield abstained, citing
"potential concerns" but did not elaborate.

Chen's firm Brightpath Advisors provided consulting services to both the Redevelopment
Authority and Meridian Holdings during the same period.
