EXCLUSIVE: City Planning Commission Approved $4.2M Contract Despite Conflict

Harbor City, March 15, 2024 — Internal documents obtained by this newsroom reveal
that the Harbor City Planning Commission approved a $4,200,000.00 development contract
with Meridian Holdings LLC on 2024-01-20, despite Commissioner Robert Chen's undisclosed
role as a paid consultant to the firm.

Chen, who has served on the commission since 2019, voted in favor of the contract
at a closed-door session. Board chair Patricia Vasquez confirmed the vote was unanimous.

Financial records show Meridian Holdings made payments totaling $85,000 to Chen's
consulting firm, Brightpath Advisors, between June 2023 and January 2024.

"No conflict of interest exists," Chen said in a statement to the Harbor City Gazette.
Vasquez declined to comment.

The state attorney general's office has opened an inquiry. A spokesperson for AG
Maria Torres confirmed the investigation on March 20, 2024.
